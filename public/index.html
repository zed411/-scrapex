FAQ

What data can I extract?
ScrapeX extracts business leads (emails, phones, LinkedIn), web search results with snippets, and YouTube video data from any search query.
How does the AI parser work?
Just type naturally like "find plumbers in Austin with email" and the AI parser auto-detects your location and keywords, then highlights them in the search fields. No complex syntax needed.
How do source toggles work?
Select which data sources to search before launching. Leads finds business contacts, Web searches Google, Video searches YouTube. Only selected sources are charged.
Can I export my data?
Yes. Click "Export CSV" in the results header to download all visible results as a CSV file.
